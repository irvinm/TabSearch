Search Contents of Loaded Tabs

How it works:
  • When you enable "Search contents of loaded tabs", the extension searches the visible text inside each open tab for your search term.
  • Tabs that contain the term remain visible; non-matching tabs are hidden.
Minimum Search Length:
  • For performance and accuracy, content search requires a minimum of 3 characters in your search term.
  • If you enter fewer than 3 characters and only content search is enabled, no tabs will be hidden or filtered.
Related Setting:
  • "Disable initial hide action" only controls a startup permission initialization step.
  • It does not change how content search matches tabs or filters results during a search.
Notes:
  • Content search only works on regular web pages (not special pages like about:blank or browser settings).
  • Tabs that are unloaded (discarded) cannot be searched until they are loaded.
  • The search is case-insensitive.
